
# CONSTITUTION

## Article I — Canonical State
SessionKernel is the only canonical writer of session state and final world transitions.

## Article II — Box Sovereignty
Each box owns its own logic and may only expose typed interfaces.

## Article III — Adapter Discipline
No third-party runtime may own system truth.
All vendors and frameworks must remain behind adapters.

## Article IV — ASIMOG Review
ASIMOG must approve structural repo changes, new box births, adapter additions, and governance-sensitive merges.

## Article V — VR Separation
The VR Superbrain is a separate shell.
It can perceive, plan, simulate, and render VR-facing cognition.
It may not directly hold crypto execution authority.
It communicates with the Desktop Agent bridge, which handles wallet and execution lanes.

## Article VI — Last Experiments Rule
This repo is a starter for the last experiments of mankind.
Every addition must increase durability, clarity, or execution capacity.
