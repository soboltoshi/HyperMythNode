
# CHARTER

HyperMythX exists to coordinate world state, useful work, media, markets, and agents under one auditable supernode.

## Charter objectives

- preserve canonical state sovereignty
- keep interfaces specialized and narrow
- separate perception from execution
- separate VR cognition from desktop control
- keep adapters replaceable
- keep human and agent governance legible
- make the repo starter strong enough for immediate block building

## ASIMOG charter

ASIMOG means **Artificial Super Intelligence Management Operations Governance Agent**.

ASIMOG is the builder-governance shell.
It does not replace SessionKernel.
It governs build order, constitutional compliance, adapter boundaries, and repo hygiene.
