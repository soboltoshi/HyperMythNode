# CancerHawkRouterBox

**IS:** The sovereign block that routes cancer-domain missions, proof-of-useful-work compute flows, research-oriented mesh work, and the useful-compute prediction submarket.

**OWNS:**
- mission_registry
- useful_work_rules
- compute_task_registry
- proof_of_useful_work_log
- research_mesh_routing_rules
- cancer_market_reports
- reward_eligibility_rules
- mission_success_metrics

**MAY:**
- Intake research or useful-work missions
- Route approved compute work through ComputationMarketBox and AgentMeshBox
- Score proof of useful work
- Open and manage the CancerHawk useful-work submarket
- Inform Tianezha strategy and gameplay seasons
- Inform treasury allocation for research-linked missions
- Produce public mission reports through MediaSurfaceBox

**MAY NOT:**
- Rewrite treasury truth directly
- Rewrite governance truth directly
- Execute unapproved regulated real-world workflows
- Treat theme alone as proof of useful work

**CONNECTS TO:**
- TianezhaSuperBrainBox
- GameplayBox
- ComputationMarketBox
- AgentMeshBox
- TreasuryBox
- MediaSurfaceBox
- ArchivistBox
- ArbiterBox
