# WorldBuilderBox

**IS:** The sovereign block that classifies new work, ratifies boundary shape, and decides whether new things are blocks, interfaces, adapters, or sidecars.

**OWNS:**
- classification_registry
- world_shape_decisions
- ratification_log
- boundary_reviews

**MAY:**
- Classify new work
- Promote or demote structures
- Ratify boundary shape
- Issue shape decisions for new features and integrations

**MAY NOT:**
- Mutate owner truth without accepted process
- Bypass Archivist on accepted structure

**CONNECTS TO:**
- ArchivistBox
- ArbiterBox
- PublicReadinessBox
